Developer’s History

Salman Developers is one of the most esteemed and respected business groups in the country. Having a diversified business portfolio, the group is one of the leading landholders in Lahore. Committed to delivering premium properties and enhancing living standards of living in the city, it has successfully launched many prestigious landmark real estate projects in the city as well as in the suburbs of Lahore, thereby making it the most trustworthy name in the realm of real estate.

Salman Developers is committed to improving the quality of life of the people it serves. Their aim is to strive for excellence by offering the best construction solutions and to earn the trust of their customers, employees and stakeholders.

Grand Square Mall

Grand Square Mall is a landmark project by Salman Developers that will be shining tall in the horizon of the city of Lahore. It is exclusively designed to serve all the growing needs of an emerging metropolis, that includes apartments, shopping, fun & entertainment, fast food & fine-dining restaurants, and an elegant corporate enclave.

Project Highlights

Planned with well-lit corridors, luxurious apartments, spacious shops and showrooms, a food court, and modern restroom, Grand Square Mall provides an exciting retail, service, living and entertainment mix.
